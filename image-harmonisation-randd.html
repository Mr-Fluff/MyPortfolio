Introduction

During my internship at ML Research Labs, I had the opportunity to contribute to the Image Harmonisation project. In this project, we aimed to enhance the process of blending foreground and background elements in image editing, creating realistic and visually appealing composite images. Let's explore the key aspects of the project and the outcomes it has achieved.

What is Image Harmonisation?

Image Harmonisation is a crucial post-processing step in image editing that focuses on seamlessly blending foreground and background elements. By effectively combining these elements, we can create composite images that appear natural and visually coherent.

Project Goals

The primary goals of the Image Harmonisation project were:

  1. Develop and optimise image harmonisation algorithms by analysing existing research.
  2. Conduct a comparative evaluation of current models to identify their strengths and weaknesses.
  3. Synthesise the findings to lay the groundwork for future advancements in the field.

Activities

I engaged in several key activities throughout the internship to achieve the project goals. These activities can be categorised into three as follows:

  1. Literature review: Conducted a comprehensive literature review to analyse the latest techniques and approaches in Image Harmonisation.
  2. Code refinement and analysis: Refactored code to industry standards and critically analysed the performance of the models using in-house and self-selected datasets.
  3. Documentation: Documented the analysis and findings, providing detailed insights into the strengths and limitations of current Image Harmonisation models.

Outcomes

This project has yielded significant outcomes, driven by my examination of various contemporary methods and focused experimentation with the Harmonizer model. These outcomes include:

  1. Identification of Limitations: Through critical analysis and rigorous testing, I uncovered limitations in the Harmonizer model related to masking techniques, foreground resolution, lighting conditions, and busy background images. This detailed exploration gave me valuable insights into the model's performance and areas for improvement.
  2. Insights into Model Performance: I better understood its feature extraction capabilities by altering the model's backbone and conducting extensive experiments. This analysis highlighted the model's strengths and weaknesses, particularly in learning lighting conditions, shadows, and image blurs.
  3. Ongoing Research for Improvement: Building on the insights gained from the limitations identified, I was actively researching alternative approaches to address these shortcomings. I explored innovative techniques to enhance the model's ability to handle challenging lighting conditions, shadows, and image blurs, aiming for more robust and accurate harmonisation results.
  4. Contributions to Advancements: The outcomes of my research contribute to the broader field of Image Harmonisation. My discoveries regarding the limitations of the Harmonizer model and ongoing research for improvement provide valuable insights for future advancements in developing more effective and sophisticated image harmonisation algorithms.

Benefits

The outcomes of the Image Harmonisation project bring significant benefits to various stakeholders, showcasing the impact and value of the research and development efforts:

  1. Project Supervisors: The comprehensive analysis conducted during the project provides Project Supervisors with valuable insights into the latest techniques and approaches in the field. This knowledge equips them to further their research and exploration, staying at the forefront of Image Harmonisation advancements.
  2. Parent Company: The optimised models developed through this project can be seamlessly integrated into the Trellis Intelligence Platform, enhancing its capabilities for future projects with other companies. This integration enables the Parent Company to offer advanced Image Harmonisation functionalities to their clients, providing a competitive edge in the market.
  3. End-Users: The refined algorithms developed during the project enable end-users to create visually appealing images that accurately represent their desired narratives. By seamlessly blending foreground and background elements, the Image Harmonisation models enhance the quality and impact of the visual content, allowing end-users to communicate their messages effectively.

Conclusion

In conclusion, my internship experience at ML Research Labs working on the Image Harmonisation project has been a highly valuable and rewarding opportunity. Through rigorous analysis of contemporary methods and focused experimentation with the Harmonizer model, I have made significant contributions and gained profound insights.

By identifying and addressing the limitations in the Harmonizer model, I have paved the way for substantial advancements in Image Harmonisation. Ongoing research efforts are dedicated to further enhancing the model's performance in handling complex lighting conditions, shadows, and image blurs, pushing the boundaries of excellence in creating seamless and visually captivating composite images.

The outcomes of this project carry substantial benefits for all stakeholders involved. Project Supervisors gain access to cutting-edge research findings, empowering them to advance their research. The Parent Company stands to gain a competitive edge by integrating the optimised models into their Trellis Intelligence Platform, offering advanced Image Harmonisation capabilities to their clients. Meanwhile, end-users can elevate their visual communication and storytelling by creating professional-looking images that precisely convey their intended messages.

Throughout this internship, my dedication to continuous improvement and innovation in the realm of Machine Learning has been unwavering. I remain enthusiastic about further exploring novel techniques and pushing the boundaries of Image Harmonisation, aiming to deliver solutions that significantly enhance the quality and impact of visual content.

Overall, this internship experience has enriched my understanding of Image Harmonisation and equipped me with valuable skills and insights that will profoundly shape my future data science and machine learning endeavors. I am grateful for the opportunity to contribute to this project and look forward to continuing my journey of professional growth.
